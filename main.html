How does it work: the text is devided into sentences and words; because the lexicon is now small and closed, detection of verbs/pronouns/nouns form is done by lists, and not with just one english tag — it is safe and effective for small amount of words. Then the sentece's structure is being built (predicate/subject/object tense, aspect, negation, question) and only then separated generator builds words form using suffixal rules and puts them in the right order.

Not available: prepositions, any english words that are not in lexicon — they will get into a category «Missing words».
